Runway

For international students competing for sponsorship in a crowded job market, Runway points to one direction worth building. Every number below traces to a public US DOL LCA filing. Your own LLM run authors the advice: Runway never calls a model and has no server.

      4 · Generate your prompt

      Runway fills a reviewer prompt with any companies you selected, your portfolio link, and your résumé's file path. You copy it and run it in your own LLM or agent (Claude, ChatGPT, …); you see every fact you send, and this page sends nothing anywhere on its own.

      Runway is career/portfolio guidance, not immigration legal advice. Data comes from public US DOL LCA disclosure files. See the provenance link above once a role is loaded.
